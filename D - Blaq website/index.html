About D-Blaq

D-BLAQ is an exciting contemporary brand for MEN inspired by Rugged Street-Swag from around the world. Designed & made proudly in Sri Lanka, with diverse styling that has created a fusion of style that is unique. Influential, innovative and progressive, D-Blaq is reinventing a wholly modern approach to fashion. Under the vision of the creative staff, the House has redefined fashion for the 21st century, further reinforcing its position as one of Sri Lanka's most desirable fashion houses. Eclectic, contemporary, D-Blaq products represent the pinnacle of Sri Lankan craftsmanship and are unsurpassed for their quality and attention to detail. We intend to change the fashion game and completely revoltunize the perception of fashion and style here in Sri Lanka. As of now we have two stores. We plan on opening many more in the near future. Our products are up to date with the global trends yet however it still has the Sri Lankan flair to it. Watch us change the game. We live in BLAQ.

Energize and enhance personality through our passion for innovative menswear solutions. Provide the best possible service to our customers by listening and acting upon their feedback.

To create a truly global brand that provides growth opportunities for the company and its employees, whilst achieving its goal of becoming the number one value fashion retailer across Sri Lanka.
